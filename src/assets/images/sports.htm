CLUBS

Cricket Club

The IIITV-ICD Cricket Club is the official cricket community of our college, bringing together passionate players and enthusiasts who share a love for the game. Our club provides a platform for students to showcase their skills, compete in college tournaments, and foster sportsmanship.

Indoor Games Club

The Indoor Club of IIITV-ICD is dedicated to promoting indoor games, primarily Carrom and Chess. The club encourages students by providing them with opportunities to enhance their skills and compete at higher levels. By fostering a competitive spirit and providing exposure to top-tier competitions, the Indoor Club plays a crucial role in shaping skilled players and representing the institute on larger platforms.
